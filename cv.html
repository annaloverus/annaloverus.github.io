Profile

Digital Strategist and behavioural scientist based in Stockholm. I have worked with technology and the Internet since 2008 – as an entrepreneur, with advertising and marketing, and as a journalist. I regularly speak about the Internet, consumer behaviours online and digital marketing strategy. I’m currently working at Spotify.

I have an academic background in behavioural science, business and economics from Uppsala University. I have also attended Copyright X (2015) by Berkman Klein Center for Internet & Society at Harvard Law School.

Swedish television, Swedish radio and newspapers such as Svenska Dagbladet, Dagens Nyheter and Metro, have all been featuring my work.
